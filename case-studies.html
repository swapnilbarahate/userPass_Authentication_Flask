Assisting startup Stact get
their product to market

THE CLIENT

Stact Tech is an innovative Australian startup offering an AI-powered risk and governance solution for the construction industry. Their solution empowers project stakeholders to confidently embark on new projects with one standardized framework across the project lifecycle, with the goal to enhance project performance.

THE CHALLENGE

Managing Director Lee Price needed to accelerate product development, while he focused his efforts on growing the business.

The AI product development team needed advice on enhancing their existing product models to prepare for market, while also building a roadmap that would guide the company’s future product plans.

To make this happen, they needed an AI expert who could provide detailed, technical guidance and offer scalable development support.

OUR SOLUTION

When Stact reached out to us for help, our global talent pool meant we were able to find the right person to lend their expertise to the team for the life of the project.

A natural fit, our AI expert had skills in data science, data engineering and product development capabilities, and was able to work individually and scale up with the larger team when needed.

It only took Stact a little over three days to onboard the new AI expert through Automatorr, resulting in a seamless transition for the team and little interruption to productivity.

THE RESULT

After just six weeks of collaboration, Lee and his in-house AI product development team were able to get their first minimum viable product (MVP) out to their construction clients. A future product roadmap was also underway.

If you want to expand your team or are looking for flexible support options to boost your existing capability, Automatorr can source the right person for the job from our global platform of extraordinary talent. Contact us today to find out more.
